### 🛠️ Tools Used
- Microsoft Excel (Pivot Tables, KPI Calculations)

- Rows: 1,000 transactions
- Columns: 9

**Task 1: Pivot Tables**

- Total Sales by Gender

- Average Age by Product Category

- Total Sales: 456,000

**Task 3: Power BI Dashboard**
Developed an interactive dashboard featuring:
- KPI Cards: Total Sales, Total Transactions, Total Quantity Sold, Average Sales
- Bar Chart: Sales by Product Category
- Pie Chart: Sales by Gender
- Column Chart: Quantity Sold by Product Category
- Line Chart: Sales Trend by Date
- Bonus Chart: Age Group vs Total Sales
- Interactive Slicers: Gender, Product Category, and Age Group

### Key Insights
1. Electronics generated the highest total revenue, closely followed by Clothing and Beauty, indicating balanced sales performance across product categories.
2. Clothing recorded the highest quantity sold despite generating less revenue than Electronics, suggesting a lower average selling price per item.
3. Female customers contributed approximately 51% of total sales, indicating an almost equal spending distribution between male and female customers.
4. The average customer age remained consistent across all product categories (around 40–42 years), suggesting that age had limited influence on purchasing preferences.
5. Sales remained relatively stable throughout the year with occasional spikes, indicating consistent demand rather than strong seasonal trends.
